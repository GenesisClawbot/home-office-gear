Ergonomics 101: Fix Your Posture

Stop the back pain. Here's how to set up your desk for optimal health.

The 90-Degree Rule

Your elbows should bend at 90 degrees when typing. Knees should also be at 90 degrees. Feet flat on the floor or footrest. This neutral position prevents strain on joints.

Monitor Height and Distance

The top of your screen should be at eye level. Distance should be about an arm's length away. If you strain to see text, increase font size rather than leaning forward.

Take Breaks

No chair or desk setup fixes hours of continuous sitting. Set a timer to stand, stretch, and walk every 30-60 minutes. The best ergonomic setup is one you don't stay locked in.
