🤔 How to Choose the Best Standing Desk for Your UK Home Office

Stability at Standing Height — This is the most important factor and the biggest differentiator between budget and premium desks. When you're typing at standing height, any wobble is distracting. Premium desks exhibit almost no wobble. Budget desks show noticeable shake, acceptable if you've never used a more stable desk.

Height Range — UK adults range from around 5'0" to 6'4". Your desk needs to go low enough for sitting with feet flat and thighs parallel to the floor, and high enough for standing with your screen at eye level. Look for 65-120cm minimum.

Motor Quality — Dual motor systems are smoother, more powerful, and more reliable than single motor systems. Look for motors with at least 80kg lifting capacity.

Desktop Size — Measure your available space. Standard UK desk widths are 100cm, 120cm, 140cm and 160cm. Depth is typically 60cm or 70cm. Dual monitor users should aim for 140cm+.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Standing Desks UK 2026

Q: Is it worth getting a standing desk in the UK?
A: Yes — if you work from home and spend 6+ hours at your desk daily. Studies show alternating between sitting and standing reduces lower back pain, improves energy levels, and can increase productivity.

Q: What is a good price for a standing desk in the UK?
A: A good quality electric standing desk in the UK costs between £250-450. Below £200, you're making compromises on motor quality and stability. Above £800, you're paying for premium aesthetics rather than functional improvements.

Q: Can I use a standing desk on carpet in my UK home?
A: Yes, but adjustable levelling feet are essential to account for uneven floors common in UK properties. Most standing desks include levelling feet.

Q: Do I need a monitor arm with a standing desk?
A: Not strictly, but we strongly recommend one. When you raise your desk to standing height, a monitor on the desktop will be at chin level. A monitor arm lets you maintain correct ergonomic positioning whether sitting or standing.
